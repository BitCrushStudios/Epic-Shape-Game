# Single Use
Med Kit - fully heal 
Mini Medkit - heal 5 hp 


# Player
Mega mushroom - increases player size 
Chain reaction - hitting enemies temporarily increases speed
Band of regen - heal 10 hp when a wave ends
Iron Plating - increase max hp by 5 
Lead weights - 40% max hp increase - 40% speed decrease 
Glass cannon - decrease health by 90% increase damage of all cubes by 200% 
New sneakers - increase player speed by 30% when close to a cube


# Cube
Icey - slow down enemies on hit 
Drill - Cube double in damage after piercing
Equal sign - each cube has a second one at their side
Controller - cubes follow your movement (takes the same path)
Tesla coil - chance to spark nearby enemies 
Crown - The other cubes tend to follow the crown cube
Coffee - Cube speed increase 10% 
Soy Milk - damage way down speed way up ( weight multiply 0.001)

## Homing Cube
Magnet - all cubes slightly home in on enemies
